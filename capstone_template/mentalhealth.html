Domestic Violence

On average 20 people/ minute are physically abused by an intimate partner in the U.S. This equals more than 10 million women and men during one year. - On a typical day more than 20 phone calls are placed to domestic violence hotlines nationwide. - Intimate partner violence accounts for 15% of all violent crime - Women between ages 18 -24 are the most common victims of domestic violence - The presence of a gun increases the risk of homicide by 500% - Ga ranks NUMBER 8 out of 10 states with the most domestic abuse cases -If called to a report of domestic violence law enforcement WOULD ALLOW guns to stay at the scene once they left because they THOUGHT the situation had diffused -65% of domestic violence related deaths in GA are related to gunshots -90% of children who are exposed to intimate partner abuse are EYEWITNESSES -Victims of domestic violence are at higher risk of developing addiction to drugs and alcohol Mental Illness -One in 3 Americans struggle with mental illness, with a higher rate being in women. -Women are 40% more likelynto develop depression, and are 2x more likely tondevelop PTSD This all comes from a traumatic experience where 1 in 4 women reported as being domestic violence or sexual assault -Trauma is a risk factor for most mental illnesses, mpost notably PTSD Drug addiction -Research has shown that physical and sexual trauma, followed by PTSD is most common in drug abusing women.
